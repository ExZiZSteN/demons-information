Description

Demons are malevolent spiritual entities that are considered the direct opposites to angels. They are created from human souls who have endured prolonged extreme torture in Hell. Via this process, they become corrupted, extremely evil, and also very powerful. Similar to Angels, they are spiritual entities and therefore require a vessel to fully interact with the physical world, though they are able to roam around in their smoke form. The species as a whole functions as the series' primary antagonists and are the most recurring supernatural creatures.

Characteristics

Demons are the result of human souls going to Hell and being tortured until no humanity is left in them, thus turning into demons - dark, malicious spirits that revel in pain, chaos, and death. Most demons foget what it means to be human.Some have even forgotten that they were once human. In general, demons engage in torture and destruction because it is fun or to avoid being tortured themselves. Some demons, however, are true believers in Lucifer's agenda to "cleanse" Earth, and actually consider themselves morally superior to humans.

There are many ways of becoming a demon, from making a deal with a Crossroad Demon, to being a witch, and making a deal with another demon. They manifest on Earth in the form of a long, slender cloud of black smoke. Once a soul becomes a demon, it gains the basic demonic powers (possession, superhuman strength, etc.) and from there, it can work its way up the ladder to positions of greater authority and power within Hell. Often when a demon has used its powers, they leave a trace of sulfur in the nearby space, or if on a person, around the victim. This can give away the demon's presence, indicating to hunters that they are hunting a demon. Very powerful demons leave much more sulfur than average. . Some demons are sent back to Earth to accomplish certain tasks, such as causing disasters, spreading diseases, assassinating hunters, or manipulating people into committing crimes that will get them sent to Hell. To do this, they have to take a vessel or "meatsuit" in order to physically exist. While nearly all demons desire to escape Hell any chance they get, some demons actually prefer Hell,

When they possess humans, they usually enter through their mouths. On one occasion, a demon entered a human via the eyes. Possessed humans often look exactly the same as they did before possession, the only noticeable change being that their eyes will sometimes change color (black, red, white, grey, or yellow depending on the demon possessing them). Demon possession also causes a chemical change in the circulatory system of the host; demon blood is known to be a unique serum and has certain attributes when ingested. Whenever they use their powers, they leave a trace of sulfur in the form of yellow dust. Angels and other supernatural beings can see the true faces of demons underneath their vessels.

Often under possession the human begins speaking and acting like the demon would. Upon exorcism, the human—if they survived—retains no memory of their time during possession (unless they have been "awake" during possession, in which case the time of possession is a nightmare for them, literally). The possessed humans also gain the powers of whichever demon is possessing them. Unlike angels, whose possession of a human vessel has to be with the vessel's consent, demons forcibly possess humans. People suffering from stress, fear or panic can be vulnerable to demonic possession. Demons abhor the name of God (Christo), and will flinch and reveal the vessels black eyes if it hears the word.

Demons apparently cannot leave a host body if they are gagged by an encrypted gag. Demons have no quarrel with killing or torturing their own kind to achieve their goals. It is revealed that demons, although being tortured and twisted human souls, can be cured.

Hierarchy

Military Hierarchy of Demons

  • White-eyed Demons - These are Lucifer's second-in-the-command in terms of military, because they are the Hell's demon chiefs of staff.
  • Yelow-eyed Demons - These are the demon army generals
  • Red-eyed Demons - These are the demon deal-makers

The Royal Hierarchy of Demons

  • Ruler of Hell - The King of Hell is the position and top title authority in Hell.
  • Princes of Hell - They are said to be the next at the throne of Hell, a claim matched only by one other demon, Lilith.
  • Ruler of the Crossroads - The King of crossroads is the position which is the Leader of all crossroads demons.
Types of Demons
  • White-eyed

    White-eyed demons are ancient demons, Lilith being the oldest of the demon kind. They're terrifyingly powerful and they rank the highest in the hierarchy of Hell acting as the demonic chiefs of staff of some sorts according to the writers. They inspire fear in their subordinates as all other demons seem to be terrified of them. Being as powerful as they are, they were resistant to many demonic weaknesses. They were even capable of holding their own against regular angels.

  • Princes of Hell/Yellow-eyed Demons

    The yellow-eyed demons are the Princes of Hell, the first generation of demons created after Lilith and the rightful rulers of Hell. The four Princes are Azazel, Ramiel, Asmodeus and Dagon. Besides having a unique durability threshold and advanced telekinesis,

  • Knight of Hell

    Knights of Hell are very powerful demons, said to be some of the first demons; handpicked by Lucifer himself, they were trained by Cain, the Father of Murder and himself the first Knight. Legend among Men of Letters had it that the archangels have destroyed all but one, but in truth Cain himself killed them. Knights possess a number of abilities not seen from other types of demons. They are also nearly invulnerable, being resilient to many defenses against demons, including exorcism rites and even the demon-killing knife. The only weapon that can kill one is the First Blade.

  • Crossroads Demons/Red-eyed Demons

    Red-eyed demons, more called Crossroads Demons, are one level higher than black-eyed demons, and have more powerful abilities, such as teleportation and deal making. Since red-eyed demons have the ability to teleport, they are usually stationed as crossroad demons, so they can teleport to the crossroads without having to be forcefully summoned. As crossroads demons, they make deals with humans, granting them one wish in exchange for their soul. In a period of time, normally ten years, a hellhound will be sent after the deal maker and drag them to Hell, where they will become a demon. While stationed as crossroad demons, red-eyed demons generally possess attractive females, presumably to attract men to make a deal. If favored by the Ruler of Hell, or another higher demon, red-eyed demons can be promoted from a crossroads demon to a higher position.

  • Black-eyed Demons

    The most common and standard class of demons on the show, black-eyed demons possess only basic powers, such as possession, super strength and telekinesis. Effective defenses against these demons include holy water, salt, iron, a Devil's Trap, etc. Stronger black-eyed demons, such as Meg or the Seven Deadly Sins, have more resistance such as not being barred from entering hallowed ground and one demon

Weaknesses

Banishing, Harming, Misleading or Trapping

  • Devil's Traps - These can bind, imprison and depower demons.
    • Devil's Trap Bags - Once forcebly used on Brady's head to make it easier to kidnap him.
    • Devil's Trap Bullets - A faster/more practical way to trap and overpower most demons.
    • Supernatural Handcuffs - A faster/more practical way to trap and overpower demons without harming or killing their vessel.
    • Fifth Pentacle of Mars - Max Banes reveals that he prefers this version of a devil's trap because it has more "character", although Sam says he uses and standard pentagram, "nothing fancy"
  • Holy Water - Holy water burns most demons that come into contact with it.
  • The Word "Christo" - The pronunciation of the name of God in latin makes nearby demons flinch and reveal their demonic eyes
  • Exorcism - Demons can be exorcised and sent back to Hell using many forms of this ritual
    • Reverse Exorcism - It can prevent a demon from leaving a vessel if they try to escape torture, interrogation, etc.
  • Jim Murphy's Knife - Even a higher black-eyed demon avoided being hit by this knife, which implies that it can at least harm or maybe even kill some demons.
  • Closing the Gates of Hell - All demons can be trapped in Hell forever (never to be summoned again) if the Trials on the Demon Tablet are undertaken.
  • Anti-Possession Sigil - It can prevent a demon from possessing someone; usually used as a tattoo by hunters.
  • Anti-Exorcism Sigil - By carving a particular sigil into a demon's possessed flesh, it is possible to lock the demon within its vessel; this can prevent a demon from escaping torture or interrogation, but also can be used by the demon to prevent themselves from being exorcised.
  • Ghosts - Ghosts can pull demons from their vessels as shown when John's spirit grabs the powerful Prince of Hell Azazel.
  • Hallowed Ground - Low level demons are unable to enter hallowed ground
  • Hex Bags - Hex bags can be used to hide people from demons. A variant also could harm and kill demons.
  • Holy Fire - Holy fire could destroy a demon's vessel and is capable of killing some of them.
  • Iron - Most demons are vulnerable to iron and contact with it burns their skin.
  • Palo Santo - Holy Wood that effects demons in the same way as holy water. It is capable of harming and immobilizing even higher level black-eyed demons like the Seven Deadly Sins, while also making it easier for them to be exorcised.
  • Demon Cure - A demon could become a human again by being subjected to this ritual as seen with Peter Kent's demon and even the Knight of Hell Dean Winchester. Unknown early versions of this ritual failed and ended up killing at least one, and presumably all 18 previous demon subjects. When left unfinished, this ritual can leave the demon with human emotions and addiction to human blood for a while as seen with Crowley. This ritual has been proved to cure Black-eyed demons, high-tier Crossroad Demons, even Knights of Hell and as Sam Winchester states, "the lore doesn’t say anything about exceptions to the cure".
    • Purified Blood - Main substance for curing demons, it is also capable of harming them.
  • Salt - Most demons are unable to cross a line of salt and could be harmed and tortured by it
    • Ingestion of Salt - Under some circumstances, the ingestion of large quantities of salt can force a demon to vacate its host
  • Hyperbolic Pulse Generators - This device emits a force that can effectively exorcise a demon or an angel from their vessel.
  • Enochian Puzzle Box - A puzzle box capable of trapping a demon within in the demon's smoke form.
  • Lilith's Crook - A horn created by Lilith to recall Hell's souls and demons back should they ever fall out of her control. Can absorb the souls, allowing the user to use them as a source of power.
Killing

Entities

Possessing the Mark of Cain is an exception to many of the beings that can kill Demons. It has been shown that only the first blade when used by a being marked with the Mark of Cain can kill another so marked. Even Death said he could not do so unless Dean passed on the Mark first.

  • Angels - Angels are capable of overpowering and smiting most demons. Higher ones can kill even the oldest demons. It is unknown if regular Angels can kill Knights or Princes, although this seems unlikely, as angels have been overpowered in many confrontations with them. At least Alastair possessed immunity to the smiting of angels.
  • Hellhounds - Hellhounds could overpower and kill demons.
  • High-tier Demons - Stronger demons can overpower or even smite lower demons
  • Leviathans - The first beasts can kill the forces of hell; the Leviathan patriarch Dick said he thought about exterminating all of demonkind from all existenc
  • Special Children - Demons are vulnerable to a special child's powers and could be controlled and killed by them. Sam Winchester was able to kill even the powerful White-eyed Demons Alastair amd Lilith
  • Cambions - A cambion, who is destined to be anti-christ, is able to immobilize, silence, exorcise and control low-to-mid level demons. It is unknown how they would fare against more powerful demons.
  • Nephilim - While empowering the Seraphim Castiel, Jack was able to grant him the power to easily overpower, render powerless and kill the Prince of Hell Dagon, one of the most powerful demons to ever live. Additionally, when Jack became angry, the Prince of Hell Asmodeus fled rather than face Jack in battle.
  • Primordial Entities - Primordial beings such as God, Amara, Death, and Archangels can easily smite any demon, including the Princes of Hell
    • God - God destroyed the Apocalypse World and all Tempter Demons within it and the mere pronunciation of God's name in latin (Christo) is enough to make demons flinch and reveal their demonic eyes while possessing someone
  • Famine - Besides Death, another horseman that has power over demons is Famine, since he can exorcise and eat demons as seen in My Bloody Valentine.

  • Angel Blades - Angel blades are capable of killing most demons. Princes have shown to be immune to Angel Blades. It is unknown if they can harm the Knights and/or White-eyed Demons.
    • Angel-Killing Bullets - Bullets forged from melted-down angel blades are capable of killing demons.
    • Archangel Blades - Archangel blades are capable of killing regular demons even in the hands of regular humans.
  • Burning their Human Remains - Burning a demon's original human bones is capable of killing them, causing both the demon in question and their vessel to burn to ash.
  • Death's Scythe - This scythe is capable of killing demons.
  • Failed Demon Curing Rituals - Unknown early versions of this ritual failed and ended up killing at least one, and presumably all 18 previous demon subjects.
  • Demon Bombs - This spell could kill demons.
  • Blood Draining - Sam and Ruby were able to kill a demon by draining all of her blood for Sam to use to fuel his powers. While under Famine's influence, Sam drained and drank the blood of two demons because of his thirst for demon blood, killing them in the process.
  • Hands of God - The power of the Hand of God is capable of reducing a demon into dust as seen when Crowley killed Simmons with the Rod of Aaron.
  • Magic - Demons could be restrained and harmed by magic, and at least two spells - one invented by the witch Rowena and the other contained in the Demon Tablet - have the proven ability to kill them.

The White-eyed Demon Alastair demonstrated a high resistance and durability to the ancient demon-killing knife of the Kurds and the demon of Halloween Samhain is implied to be too powerful for Ruby's knife to kill him.
  • The Colt - Since this gun can kill anything but Lucifer and other four beings in the existence, it's capable of killing demons including the Princes of Hell.
  • The First Blade - The First Blade, combined with power from the Mark of Cain, is capable of killing demons.
  • Lucifer's Spear - Lucifer briefly threatened Crowley with it, forcing the blade of the spear against his neck while taunting him.
  • Lance of Michael - Angelic weapon created by the archangel Michael to slowly and painfully kill Lucifer. Will kill any demon stabbed with it causing them to explode into dust. It was able to kill the very powerful Prince of Hell Ramiel.
  • Virgin Sacrifice Spell (possibly) - Ruby claimed to know a spell that would destroy any demon within a one-mile radius
